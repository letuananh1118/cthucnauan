Scrambled eggs

Scrambled eggs is popular food that everyone likes it

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1tbs butter
  • 2 tbs cream

Methods

  1. Melt butter in a flying pan over a medium heat
  2. Gently mix the eggs and cream in a bowl
  3. Once butter has melted add cream and eggs
  4. Using a spatula fold the eggs from the edge of the pan to the center every 20s
  5. When the eggs are still moist remove from the heat(it will continue to cook on the plated until served)
